About Us

We are Falkon Robotics, a high school team who’ve been competing and educating all those interested since 2001.

We have an open-door policy where there is no minimum attendance, no applications, and no fee to join or participate.

Our goal is to educate people about STEAM (science, technology, engineering, art, and math) in a fun and engaging way.

We are a global non profit organization with programs in 100+ countries

We even have a 501(3)(c) non profit organization! Friends of falkon robotics is a non-profit organization established by previous alumni, dedicated to our mission.

Our Impact

This year alone we have 12 FLL teams with 76 4th - 8th grade students and 73 high school mentors very close to one on one mentoring.

Raised 12000$ + in order to purchase 32 LEGO Robotics kits for community schools

This year alone we hosted a mock competition for 29 teams with over 200 attendees.

In the past we’ve hosted engineering expos (the event you're in right now) 2 times and we’ve had 1500+ attendees.

85% of our alumni go on to study and work in STEAM fields (compared to 64% average). 80% of Falkon Robotics alumnae go into STEAM fields (2.4x the national average, which is about 33%).
