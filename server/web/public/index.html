        About Us

        We are Falkon Robotics, a high school team who've been competing and educating all those interested since 2001.

        We have an open-door policy where there is no minimum attendance, no applications, and no fee to join or participate.

        Our goal is to educate people about STEAM in an open and engaging way.

        We even have a 501(3)(c) non profit organization! Friends of Falkon Robotics is a non-profit organization established by previous alumni, dedicated to our mission.

        Falkon Robotics members share passion in volunteering for various community events and initiatives for our non profit organization.

        We don't just build good robots, we build good people.


        Our Impact

        This year alone we have...

        - Mentored 76 4th - 8th grade students to compete in competition-styled robotics matches.

        - Raised $12,000+ in order to purchase essential STEAM education resources for local schools.

        - Hosted robotics competitions for 200+ elementary & middle school attendees, strengthening STEAM and presentation skills for attendees.

        - Held Engineering Expos, like the one you're at, at no cost for 1500+ attendees.

        - 80% of previous Falkon Robotics alumni went into STEAM fields (2.4x the national average, which is about 33%).

        And way more!
